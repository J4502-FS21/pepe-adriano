Hi, I'm Pepe.

I'm a Mexican journalist currently based in Missouri, USA. I have experience covering technology, politics, telecommunications, consumer electronics, cybersecurity, startups, among other topics. While reporting for news outlets, I have traveled to South Korea, Germany, Spain, Canada, France, and the US, covering events such as CES, MWC, IFA, and Google I/O.

I'm a current Journalism Master's student at the University of Missouri, Class of 2022. While studying, I've collaborated as a State Government Reporter with the Columbia Missourian, and as a RJI's Innovation Fellow at the Detroit Free Press.

By the way, you can call me Pepe.
